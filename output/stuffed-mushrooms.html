Stuffed Mushrooms

Thu 06 January 2022
By Patrick Feeney
Category: American

Ingredients

  • 8 oz baby bella mushrooms
  • 3/4 stick of butter
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • Sprinkle of parmesan

Steps

  1. Hollow out mushrooms and dice the mushroom stems.
  2. Melt butter in pan, then sautee mushroom stems.
  3. Sautee garlic when stems are done.
  4. Mix bread crumbs and parmesan into pan and fill mushrooms with the mixture.
  5. Bake mushrooms for 15 minutes at 350 degrees.

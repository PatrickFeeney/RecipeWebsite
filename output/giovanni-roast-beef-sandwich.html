Giovanni Roast Beef Sandwich

Fri 07 April 2023
By Patrick Feeney
Category: American

Ingredients

  • Ciabatta roll
  • Sliced roast beef
  • Swiss cheese
  • Sliced red onion
  • Roasted red peppers
  • Red pepper pesto

Steps

  1. Toast outsides of bun under broiler, assemble sandwich open-faced, and toast under the broiler again. Alternatively, assemble sandwich then toast in a panini press.

Nana's Meat Sauce

Sat 11 December 2021
By Patrick Feeney
Category: Italian

Ingredients

  • 52 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 24 ounces tomato paste
  • 24 ounces water
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 4 tsp sugar

  • 4-6 bay leaves

  • 2 baseball sized onions, diced

  • 2 tsp or cloves minced garlic
  • olive oil
  • 1 lbs ground beef

Steps

  1. Saute onion in olive oil in sauce pan. Quickly saute garlic once onion is almost translucent.
  2. Brown ground beef in another pan and drain fat. Add to sauce pan.
  3. Add remaining ingredients to sauce pan. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to simmer for at least 45 minutes. Partly cover to avoid splattering.

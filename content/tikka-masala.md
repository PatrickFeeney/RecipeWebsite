### Ingredients

For marinade:

- 5.3 oz plain Greek yogurt
- 1 Tbs grated ginger
- 2 cloves grated garlic
- 4 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp kashmiri red chili powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
- 8 boneless skinless chicken thighs (about 2.5 lbs)

For sauce:

- 1 onion sliced
- 2 jalepe√±o or serrano peppers, stemmed, seeded, and chopped
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 Tbs ginger, grated or sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, grated or smashed
- 4 Tbs ghee or butter
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 2 tsp dried fenugreek leaves
- 1 tsp kashmiri red chili powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- 28 ounce canned tomatos, whole peeled or crushed
- (optional) 1/4 cup heavy cream
- (optional) cilantro for garnish

### Steps

1. Combine marinade ingredients in a bowl then toss chicken and refrigerate for 3-24 hours.
1. Melt butter over medium heat in an immersion blender safe pot. Saute onions and peppers with salt for 8-10 minutes. Add garlic and ginger, sauting for 2-3 minutes. Add remaining spices and sautee for 1 minute. Add tomatoes, breaking with spoon if whole, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 30 minutes or until sauce has reduced by half.
1. Use immersion blender until sauce is smooth, angling pot to reduce splashes if needed. Add cream if using.
1. Put pot over medium heat and add chicken. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to simmer. Continue until chicken cooked, roughly 15-20 minutes. Add salt to taste.

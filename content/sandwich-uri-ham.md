### Ingredients

- Ciabatta roll
- Sliced ham
- Swiss cheese
- Baby spinach
- Red pepper pesto

### Steps

1. Toast outsides of bun under broiler, assemble sandwich open-faced, and toast under the broiler again. Alternatively, assemble sandwich then toast in a panini press.

Munchies Coleslaw

Ingredients

  • 1/2 head cabbage
  • 1/2 handful chopped fresh parsley

For vinaigrette:

  • 4 pinches celery seed
  • 3 Tbs white vinegar
  • 1 Tbs sugar
  • 1/2 cup canola oil

Steps

  1. Remove core of cabbage and cut once for 2 1/4 cabbage chunks. Lay chunks with outside of the cabbage up and slice very thinly.

  2. Mix vinaigrette ingredients together in a bowl.

  3. Add cabbage and parsley to a large bowl. Slowly mix in vinaigrette in small batches until tasty, reserving leftover vinaigrette.
